What kind of work is done by the IT professional?

- Lucy Webster, she is a senior cloud engineer at Itoc, AWS Advanced Consulting Partner. They do 24/7 support, maintenance and backups for their customers. They also monitor their entire system using the platform, Terraformed and automated tool, GitHub Actions.

What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with? Are they other IT professionals? Clients? Investors? The general public?

Where does the IT professional spend most of their time?

- She spends most of her time at her office

What aspect of their position is most challenging?
